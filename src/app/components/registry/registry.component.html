Our Italian Adventure

The morning after we say our vows, we leave for Italy.

It has always been a place we have wanted to explore together — the kind of country that feels like it was made for newlyweds. We plan to travel the length of it, taking our time and soaking it all in. From the ancient streets of Rome and the art-filled galleries of Florence, north to the romance of Venice with its candlelit canals and crumbling palazzos, and south to the dramatic cliffs and turquoise waters of the Amalfi Coast.

We want long lunches that stretch into evenings, glasses of wine in tiny trattorias, golden hillsides in Tuscany, and the kind of slow, unhurried days that only happen when you're completely free.

We'll be adding photos from the trip to this page when we return — so check back in and share the adventure with us.
